      Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

      1. Am I eligible For Lasik/Contoura Vision Treatment ?

      Generally, a suitable candidate will be at least 18 years of age, have had a stable vision for the last 12 months, is not pregnant, is free of certain diseases of the cornea and retina and is generally in good health.

      2. How soon can I get back to normal, drive, play sport or go to work?

      Most people return to work and drive the day after lasik surgery.

      3. Is the procedure safe?

      LASIK is considered to be very safe. Over 10 million cases have been performed worldwide in the last decade.

      4.Will my vision be corrected forever?

      Once the cornea has been modified it tends to stay modified permanently. The vast majority of corrected eyes remain stable, permanently correcting near-sightedness, far-sightedness and astigmatism.

      5. How will I know which treatment is best for me ?

      Your surgeon will advise this after a thorough examination at a consultation.

      6. Are Pristyn Care Ophthalmologists Reliable?

      Yes, ophthalmologists at Pristyn Care are highly reliable. They come with years of experience of treating different eye problems for men and women of all age groups.
